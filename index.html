The doctor

Twenty-seven years of steady, attentive care.

Dr. Pentyala is a board-certified internal medicine physician who has cared for adults in and around Livingston since the practice opened. Her approach is straightforward: prevent what she can, catch what she can't early, and stay with her patients through the conditions that need a steady hand.

She trained at Guntur Medical College and completed her residency at Monmouth Medical Center in Long Branch, NJ. She is affiliated with Cooperman Barnabas Medical Center.
